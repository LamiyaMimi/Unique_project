Water lily is our national flowers

White water lily

Water lilies are beautiful aquatic plants that float gracefully on the surface of still water. They belong to the family Nymphaeaceae and are known for their broad, round leaves and stunning flowers that come in various colors like white, pink, yellow, and purple. Each flower usually opens in the morning and closes by evening.

❮ ❯

Pink water lily

Pink water lilies are especially striking with their soft to vibrant pink petals floating atop green lily pads. They are often associated with love, tranquility, and beauty. These flowers thrive in ponds, lakes, or slow-moving streams and bloom during warm weather, adding a serene charm to aquatic environments.

❮ ❯

Violet water lily

The violet water lily is a captivating aquatic flower with rich violet or purple petals that radiate elegance. It often symbolizes peace, creativity, and spiritual growth. Like other water lilies, it floats on still water, with its bright petals contrasting beautifully against the green lily pads and often a yellow or orange center.
